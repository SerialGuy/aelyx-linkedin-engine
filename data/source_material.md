## Content angles to rotate through

1. **Case study / client win** — specific problem → specific Aelyx solution →
   specific measurable outcome.
2. **Product education** — what a specific Aelyx product actually does, in plain
   language, framed around the buyer's pain point.
3. **Thought leadership / AI insight** — broader observation about AI in business,
   grounded in something Aelyx has actually built or seen, not generic "AI trends"
   commentary.
4. **Behind-the-build** — a real technical challenge Aelyx solved (e.g., the LDE
   constrained-language problem, the IoT RPM guarantee) — appeals to technical
   buyers and shows depth competitors can't fake.
5. **Founder perspective** — Aelyx's own take on building an AI company in India
   serving both Indian SMBs and the US market — credibility + relatability angle.
